Elephant

The largest land animal on Earth, elephants use their trunks for breathing, smelling, drinking, and grabbing objects.

Elephants are known for their intelligence, strong family bonds, and long memories. They are herbivores, spending up to 16 hours a day eating grasses, fruit, and bark. Their iconic tusks are actually elongated incisor teeth.
